## Idea Generation Process

1. **Review existing content**
   - Check `.claude/linkedin-ai-voice.local.md` for:
     - Previously covered topics (avoid repetition)
     - Ideas backlog (build on existing ideas)
     - Engagement learnings (what resonated)

2. **Research current trends** (if WebSearch available)
   - Search for latest AI news and announcements
   - Check what AI topics are trending on Twitter/X
   - Look for industry reports or surveys to reference
   - Identify controversial takes generating discussion

   - Idea + suggested hook

4. **Apply topic diversity**
   Ensure ideas span multiple core areas:
   - [ ] AI Adoption in Enterprise
   - [ ] AI Readiness for Organizations
   - [ ] Organization Structure for AI
   - [ ] AI at Scale
   - [ ] AI Strategy/Ethics
   - [ ] AI Industry News

5. **Rank by timeliness**
   - **Post this week**: Time-sensitive ideas
   - **Evergreen**: Can post anytime
   - **Save for later**: Need more research or development

6. **Save to ideas backlog**
   - Append new ideas to `.claude/linkedin-ai-voice.local.md` under "Ideas Backlog"
   - Include suggested format and hook for each

## Idea Quality Criteria

Each idea should pass:
- [ ] Specific enough to write a hook for
- [ ] Relevant to enterprise AI audience
- [ ] Aligned with strategic advisor + pragmatic executive voice
- [ ] Not recently covered
- [ ] Has a clear insight or takeaway
