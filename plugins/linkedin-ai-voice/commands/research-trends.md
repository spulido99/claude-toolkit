Research the current AI landscape to identify timely content opportunities.

## Research Process

1. **Search AI news sources**
   Use WebSearch to find recent AI news from:
   - Tech news: TechCrunch, The Verge, Wired, VentureBeat, Ars Technica
   - AI-specific: AI newsletters, research blogs, arxiv summaries
   - Business/Enterprise: HBR, McKinsey, Gartner, Forrester
   - Social: Twitter/X AI discussions, trending topics

   Search queries to run:
   - "AI news this week"
   - "enterprise AI trends 2025"
   - "AI adoption challenges"
   - "AI announcements" + current date
   - "generative AI enterprise"

2. **Categorize findings**

   ### Breaking News
   Major announcements, funding rounds, product launches
   - [Finding 1]: [Source] - [Your angle]
   - [Finding 2]: [Source] - [Your angle]

   ### Trending Discussions
   Hot debates, controversial takes, viral threads
   - [Finding 1]: [Source] - [Your angle]
   - [Finding 2]: [Source] - [Your angle]

   ### Research & Reports
   New studies, surveys, analyst reports
   - [Finding 1]: [Source] - [Your angle]
   - [Finding 2]: [Source] - [Your angle]

   ### Enterprise AI Moves
   Company AI strategies, implementations, case studies
   - [Finding 1]: [Source] - [Your angle]
   - [Finding 2]: [Source] - [Your angle]

3. **Assess content potential**

   For each finding, evaluate:
   - **Timeliness**: Is this news fresh enough to comment on?
   - **Relevance**: Does this matter to enterprise AI leaders?
   - **Angle opportunity**: What unique perspective can you add?
   - **Format fit**: Story, list, hot take, or news commentary?

4. **Generate content opportunities**

   Present top 5 opportunities:

   | Topic | Source | Timeliness | Suggested Angle | Format |
   |-------|--------|------------|-----------------|--------|
   | [Topic 1] | [Source] | [Fresh/Aging/Evergreen] | [Your unique take] | [Format] |
   | [Topic 2] | [Source] | [Fresh/Aging/Evergreen] | [Your unique take] | [Format] |
   | ... | ... | ... | ... | ... |

5. **Draft hook ideas**
   For top 3 opportunities, provide:
   - Suggested hook (using patterns from linkedin-ai-strategy skill)
   - Key insight to highlight
   - Potential CTA

6. **Save research**
   - Append findings to `.claude/linkedin-ai-voice.local.md` under "Research Notes"
   - Add promising topics to "Ideas Backlog"
   - Note sources for future reference

## Research Focus Areas

Prioritize research on:
1. **AI Adoption**: Rollout stories, change management, stakeholder buy-in
2. **AI Readiness**: Assessment frameworks, capability gaps
3. **Org Structure**: Team composition, CoE models, Chief AI Officer role
4. **AI at Scale**: Pilot-to-production, governance, MLOps
5. **AI Ethics/Strategy**: Responsible AI, regulations, business transformation
6. **Industry News**: Funding, launches, partnerships, research breakthroughs
